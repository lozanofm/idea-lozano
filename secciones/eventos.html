COMPETITION RULES

In order to be eligible for a prize, athletes must have submitted videos of their performances following the rules of each individual challenge completed during the corresponding challenge time frame. If a competitor has not submitted a video during the challenge window, they will remain on the individual event leaderboard but not count toward the overall leaderboard Athletes can not submit a video following the close of the challenge window Videos will not be requested at a later date or during the final overall leaderboard judging/finalization process Overall Challenge is open to RX'd athletes only. Scores on the overall leaderboard are unofficial until winners are announced following the completion of the 2022 Challenge season 1st place receives 100 points, 2nd place receives 99 points - down to 98th place and lower receive 2 points. If athletes register but do not submit a score, they will receive 0 points
